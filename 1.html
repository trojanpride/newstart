HTML이란 웹페이지와 웹애플리케이션을 만드는 표준언어이다.

HyperText is the method by which you move around on the web — by clicking on special text called hyperlinks which bring you to the next page.

The fact that it is hyper just means it is not linear — i.e. you can go to any place on the Internet whenever yo u
want by clicking on links — there is no set order to do things in.
